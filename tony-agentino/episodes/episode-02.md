# Tony Agentino — Episode 2
## "Frank's First Day"

**Length:** ~4 minutes  
**Format:** Cold open (Tony in therapy) → Cutaway → Therapy → Cutaway → Therapy closing → Cold close

---

### COLD OPEN

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

Tony sits in the leather chair. He's early. He brought coffee. He sets it on her desk. She raises an eyebrow.

**DR. RUSSO**
That's new.

**TONY**
Couldn't sleep. Figured I'd be useful.

**DR. RUSSO**
You didn't sleep?

**TONY**
I had a... a personnel issue. Someone new started at work. Well. Not new-new. New to the territory.

**DR. RUSSO**
A competitor?

**TONY**
(small smile)
You could say that. He came in from Queens. Very aggressive. Good trucks. New trucks. Mine are... established.

**DR. RUSSO**
That sounds stressful.

**TONY**
It's business. You expect competition. What I didn't expect was that he didn't know who he was competing with.

**DR. RUSSO**
You mean he didn't do his research?

**TONY**
He did research. He just didn't understand it.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. AGENTINO SANITATION - DAY**

A man — VINNY "THE TRUCK" MORETTI (late 30s, loud, flashy — the kind of guy who wears a polo with the collar up) — walks into Agentino Sanitation like he owns the place.

He's got a folder. A big folder.

Tony's at his desk. Doesn't look up.

**VINNY**
Tony Agentino?

**TONY**
Who's asking?

**VINNY**
Vinny Moretti. I bought the route on 42nd. You know, the one you "lost" in the bidding?

Tony looks up. Studies Vinny. Sees the polo. The collar. The confidence.

**TONY**
I didn't lose anything.

**VINNY**
(smug)
Well, it's mine now. All of it. And I'm going to be taking some of your other contracts too. You can either make this easy, or—

**TONY**
Or?

**VINNY**
Let's just say my trucks are louder than yours.

Tony is quiet for a moment.

**TONY**
Sal. Get the coffee.

SAL enters from the back. He's holding two cups. He looks at Vinny. He doesn't like him.

**SAL**
Boss?

**TONY**
Set them down.

Sal puts the cups on the desk. Tony pushes one toward Vinny.

**TONY**
You want my routes? Have a cup of coffee. Then I'll tell you everything you need to know.

VINNY
(sitting down, smug)
Now we're talking.

He takes a sip.

**TONY**
(smiling)
Good?

**VINNY**
Not bad.

**TONY**
That's my mother's recipe. She taught me — you catch more flies with honey than vinegar.

Vinny's face changes. The room gets quiet.

**VINNY**
What did you just—

**TONY**
Sal. Close the door.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. TANK ROOM - NIGHT**

Sal stands in front of the tank. Tony's beside him. They both stare at the water.

The hum. It fills the room.

**SAL**
Not Frank.

**TONY**
Sal—

**SAL**
Tony. He just got here. He hasn't even — look at him. He's just sitting there. He doesn't even know what's coming.

Tony looks at Frank. Frank is, indeed, just sitting there.

**TONY**
Vinny Moretti threatened my family. My people. He came into my building and he sat in my chair and he thought—

**SAL**
I know. I know. But Frank — Frank's different.

**TONY**
They're all different, Sal.

**SAL**
No. Listen to me. This one — this one looks at you. Like he understands. I've never seen a lobster like this.

**TONY**
(beat)
It's a lobster, Sal.

**SAL**
(quietly)
So was Giuseppe. And you know what happened to Giuseppe.

A long silence.

**TONY**
(sighing)
Fine. We'll use Vinny first. But if there's a next time—

**SAL**
(quickly)
There won't be. I'll talk to Frank. He'll understand.

Tony shakes his head. Leaves the room.

Sal leans close to the tank.

**SAL**
(whispering)
You're safe, Frank. For now. But you gotta be good. You gotta be good.

Frank does not respond. He is a lobster.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

**DR. RUSSO**
You seem distracted today.

**TONY**
Just thinking about someone at work. He's — he's a good worker. Loyal. But he gets too attached to things that don't matter.

**DR. RUSSO**
What doesn't matter?

**TONY**
Outcomes. End results. He thinks things should be fair. That people should get what they deserve.

**DR. RUSSO**
And you don't?

**TONY**
I think fairness is a word people use when they don't understand the system.

**DR. RUSSO**
What system is that?

**TONY**
The one where everyone has a role. You do yours. I do mine. And sometimes—sometimes someone doesn't do what they're supposed to. And it falls on everyone else.

**DR. RUSSO**
That sounds like a lot of responsibility.

**TONY**
It's what the job requires.

**DR. RUSSO**
Do you ever think about changing jobs?

**TONY**
(small laugh)
Every day.

**DR. RUSSO**
But you don't.

**TONY**
You can't walk away from something you've built. You'd be leaving other people to deal with the mess.

**DR. RUSSO**
The mess.

**TONY**
The mess.

(beat)

What happened with this Vinny guy? In your story?

**DR. RUSSO**
(she said "story"?)
I didn't say it was a story.

**TONY**
You didn't not say it.

She laughs. He doesn't.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. WAREHOUSE - NIGHT**

Vinny "The Truck" Moretti is duct-taped to a chair. Same room as before. The hum is louder now.

He looks around. Sees the tank. The water. The lobsters.

**VINNY**
What — what is this? What the fuck is this?!

Tony stands in front of him. Calm. Measured.

**TONY**
Vinny. Let me tell you about the waste management business.

**VINNY**
Please — I have a family —

**TONY**
Everyone has a family.

**VINNY**
I'll sign anything —

**TONY**
You're going to sign the 42nd Street contract back to me. And you're going to close your operation. Quietly. By Friday.

**VINNY**
And then?

**TONY**
And then you go back to Queens. You find a different business. Something away from here. Away from my routes. And you remember this conversation.

**VINNY**
I will. I swear.

**TONY**
I know you will.

The chair rolls forward. Into the darkness.

Splash. Screaming. Then nothing.

---

**INT. TANK ROOM - LATER**

Sal is feeding the lobsters. Tony walks in.

**SAL**
He's still in there.

**TONY**
He'll talk. Once he realizes we're serious.

**SAL**
No — Vinny. He's still in there. Hasn't been pulled out yet.

**TONY**
I know.

**SAL**
Why?

**TONY**
Because he's not done screaming.

Sal looks at the tank. Then at Tony.

**SAL**
You're a cold motherfucker, you know that?

**TONY**
(small smile)
Go home, Sal.

**SAL**
Frank's waiting for his dinner.

**TONY**
I know. Give him extra tonight.

Sal nods. He leaves.

Tony stands alone in front of the tank. The water glows faintly. Blue.

He puts his hand on the glass.

**TONY**
(quietly)
Not you. Never you.

The lobsters move. Frank sits still.

---

### COLD CLOSE

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

**DR. RUSSO**
You said "not you" just now. In the middle of my question.

**TONY**
Did I?

**DR. RUSSO**
You did. Then you went somewhere else.

**TONY**
Sorry. I have a — a thing. At work.

**DR. RUSSO**
Something with the new person?

**TONY**
The new person is gone. But there's someone else. Someone who's been there a while.

**DR. RUSSO**
And you don't want to lose them?

**TONY**
(quietly)
I don't want to have to use them.

She writes something down. Looks at him.

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony. What are you not telling me?

He stands. Puts on his jacket.

**TONY**
That I had a good week, Doctor. That's all.

He leaves.

She stares at the door. Then at her notebook.

She flips back through the pages. Every session. Every metaphor.

Her phone buzzes. She looks at it.

Text from a colleague: "Hey, you still seeing that sanitation guy? My husband knows someone who does contract work for the city. Says there's rumors about some guys on the sanitation side. You be careful."

She puts the phone down.

---

**EXT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

Tony walks out. Stops on the sidewalk.

Takes out his phone. Texts Sal: "Frank's good. No one touches him."

Walks to his Cadillac. Gets in.

Sits there.

Looks at his arm. The sore spot.

On the dashboard: the wedding photo. Same one.

He starts the car.

Pulls out.

---

**TITLE CARD:**

*"The mess is always there. You just move it around."*

**TONY AGENTINO**
**Episode 2: Frank's First Day**

- **Dr. Russo's suspicion:** She gets a text. Someone's talking about Tony. She's starting to dig, even unknowingly
- **The tank:** We see more of it this time. The glow is blue now. Subtle shift.
- **Tony's arm:** Still sore. Still rubbing it. Still won't explain.
- **End of episode:** Tony protects Frank. Vinny's still in the tank. "He's not done screaming."
- **Tease for Episode 3:** Dr. Russo is getting suspicious. Someone's asking about Tony.
