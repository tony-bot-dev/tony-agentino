# Tony Agentino 🎬🦞

An original animated series pitch — dark comedy about a guilt-ridden mobster who runs a waste management empire in New Jersey and uses a basement full of clawless lobsters as his preferred method of "persuasion."

## The Pitch

> "A guilt-ridden Italian-American mobster runs a waste management empire in New Jersey, struggles with a therapist who can't know his real job, and disposes of his problems — one lobster tank at a time."

**Logline:** "The mess is always there. You just move it around."

## Format

3-5 minute YouTube shorts — each episode is a therapy session where Tony describes what happened that week in the most beautiful, indirect language possible. Dr. Russo responds. The audience sees the actual events in cutaways.

## Tone

- Dark Comedy
- Character Drama
- Adult Animation
- BoJack Horseman DNA
- Italian-American
- Crime + Therapy Sessions

## Characters

- **Tony Agentino** — 42, beautiful suits, terrible at lying to himself, runs Agentino Sanitation Solutions (mob front). Refuses to use guns. The lobster tank is his mercy.
- **Dr. Carla Russo** — Therapist, increasingly suspicious of Tony's "corporate logistics" job
- **Sal** — Cousin, manages the tank, names all the lobsters, gets attached
- **Donna Agentino** — Mother, 70s, tiny, terrifying, knows everything, acknowledges nothing
- **Marco** — Tony's protégé, too smart, too moral — a mirror Tony can't stop looking at
- **The Tank** — Basement, hums, glows, lives beneath the restaurant
