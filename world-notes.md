# Tony Agentino — Additional World Notes

## The Tank Mythology

The tank isn't just lobsters. It's been in the family for three generations.

- **Tony's grandfather** — brought it over from the old country. Used it for "the serious problems." Never talked about it.
- **Tony's father** — expanded the operation. Added the filtration system. The glow started showing up near the end.
- **Tony** — inherited it. Added the clawless modification ( Vet who owed him a favor, "a small procedure"). Believed it was more "humane."

The glow? Nobody knows. Sal thinks it's spiritual. Marco thinks it's a chemical imbalance. Tony thinks it's probably a light fixture he forgot to turn off.

## Why Clawless?

Tony made that call. His logic: "A claw is a weapon. Weapons are for people who've made choices. These are just... consequences without agency."

He tells this to Dr. Russo in Episode 3. She says "That sounds like you're describing yourself." He doesn't answer for a long time.

## Dr. Russo's Arc

- **Episodes 1-3:** Skeptical but charmed
- **Episodes 4-5:** Noticing patterns
- **Episode 6:** Realizes Tony's "corporate logistics" is not corporate
- **Episode 7:** Pieces together enough to be dangerous
- **Episode 8:** Makes a choice about what to do with what she knows

She never finds out about the tank. Not in Season 1.

## Sal's Lobster Registry

Sal keeps a notebook. Every lobster has:
- Name
- Date of arrival
- "Personality assessment"
- "Tank readiness" (1-10)
- "Emotional attachment level" (1-10 — Sal's always at 10)

Notable names across seasons:
- Frank (the famous one)
- Carmine
- "That One We Don't Talk About"
- The Administrator (a lobster who just sat there)
- Giuseppe (Sal's favorite, lived 4 years)
- Several unnamed ones Sal refuses to name because "naming is a promise"

## Running Gag: Tony's Car

Tony drives a 2019 Cadillac Escalade. Black. Spotless. It appears in every cold open. It's the only thing in his life that's simple.

## Running Gag: The Infomercials

Tony can't sleep. He watches infomercials at 2am. Cutaway after every intense scene:
- "WOULD YOU LIKE TO SLEEP BETTER?"
- "TIRED OF STAINING? THIS PRODUCT CHANGES EVERYTHING."
- "CALL NOW AND RECEIVE A FREE SECOND SET."

He never buys anything.

## The Code (Tony's Version)

1. Never use a gun. Guns are final. The tank is a warning.
2. Always pull them out. Everyone gets a second chance.
3. Never touch women or children. Not a rule, just something he won't do.
4. Family is family. Blood or otherwise.
5. The tank hums. When it stops humming, something's wrong.
