# Tony Agentino — Episode 6
## "Marco's Choice"

**Length:** ~4 minutes  
**Format:** Cold open (Tony in therapy) → Cutaway → Therapy → Cutaway → Therapy → Cutaway → Cold close

---

### COLD OPEN

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

Tony sits in the chair. He's calm. Too calm. The kind of calm that comes after the storm.

**DR. RUSSO**
You seem different today.

**TONY**
Do I?

**DR. RUSSO**
Last week you were — you were scared. I could see it. Now you seem... resolved.

**TONY**
(small smile)
I had a talk with someone. About something I'd been avoiding.

**DR. RUSSO**
A talk with who?

**TONY**
With my — with someone I mentored. Someone I brought into the business.

**DR. RUSSO**
And how did that go?

**TONY**
(pause)
It didn't go. It already happened.

**DR. RUSSO**
I'm not following.

**TONY**
Someone made a choice. On my behalf. Without asking. And now I have to live with it.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. AGENTINO SANITATION - DAY - ONE WEEK EARLIER**

Tony's office. Marco sits across from him. His face is stone.

**MARCO**
It's my brother.

**TONY**
What about him?

**MARCO**
He got picked up. Last night. Federal.

Tony leans back.

**TONY**
Doing what?

**MARCO**
You know what.

**TONY**
I need to hear you say it.

**MARCO**
(slowly)
He was running product. For one of my guys. Someone I trusted. And he got picked up. And he's — he's going to flip.

**TONY**
Flip on who?

**MARCO**
Everyone. Me. You. The whole operation.

**TONY**
How long does he have?

**MARCO**
They're offering him a deal. 72 hours.

**TONY**
Then we cut our losses.

**MARCO**
What?

**TONY**
We cut him loose. New identity. Somewhere far away. It costs us, but—

**MARCO**
He won't go.

**TONY**
He'll go.

**MARCO**
He has a family. Kids. He won't leave them.

**TONY**
Then he stays and we all go down.

**MARCO**
(standing)
I can't let that happen.

**TONY**
Then what? Huh? What are you going to do?

A long silence.

**MARCO**
I'm going to handle it.

**TONY**
Handle it how?

**MARCO**
The way you taught me.

**TONY**
I never taught you anything like that.

**MARCO**
You didn't have to.

Marco leaves.

Tony sits there. Alone.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

**DR. RUSSO**
This person you mentored. You said they made a choice.

**TONY**
They did.

**DR. RUSSO**
Without asking.

**TONY**
Without asking.

**DR. RUSSO**
What kind of choice?

**TONY**
The kind you can't take back.

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony. What did they do?

**TONY**
(looking at his hands)
They solved a problem.

**DR. RUSSO**
A problem?

**TONY**
A person. A person who was going to — who was going to hurt us. Hurt everyone. And they solved it.

**DR. RUSSO**
Solve how?

**TONY**
(very quietly)
The way we solve things.

**DR. RUSSO**
I don't understand.

**TONY**
No. You don't.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. WAREHOUSE - NIGHT**

A man — MARCO'S BROTHER, MIKE (early 30s, scared, looking at his brother like he's a stranger) — is duct-taped to a chair. The tank is behind him. The glow is blue. The hum fills the room.

Marco stands in front of him.

**MIKE**
Marco. Marco, please. I'm your brother. I'm your brother!

**MARCO**
I know who you are.

**MIKE**
Then let me go! I'll run. I'll — I'll do whatever you want. Just—

**MARCO**
You're going to flip. On everyone. On me. On Tony. On everyone who's ever given us a job.

**MIKE**
I have kids! I have a family!

**MARCO**
I have a family too.

**MIKE**
Please!

**MARCO**
(calmly)
You're going to tell me everything. Every deal. Every name. Every number. And then you're going to walk out of here.

**MIKE**
You — you'll let me go?

**MARCO**
I'll let you go.

Mike starts talking. Everything. Every deal. Every name. For six hours.

Marco listens. Writes everything down.

Then he stands.

**MARCO**
You done?

**MIKE**
Yeah. Yeah, I'm done. Please — let me go.

**MARCO**
I said I'd let you go.

He walks to the tank. Pulls a lever.

The chair rolls forward.

Mike's eyes go wide.

**MIKE**
NO! NO! YOU SAID — YOU SAID YOU'D LET ME GO!

**MARCO**
I am. You're going in. And then you're coming out. Just like Tony taught us. Everyone gets a second chance.

**MIKE**
I DON'T WANT A SECOND CHANCE!

**MARCO**
Too bad.

The chair goes into the tank.

Splash. Screaming. Then nothing.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. TANK ROOM - LATER**

Mike is pulled out. Coughing. Shaking. Alive.

Marco stands over him.

**MARCO**
You're free to go.

**MIKE**
(terrified)
What — what do I do now?

**MARCO**
You go home. You kiss your kids. And you never — never contact the feds again. Or the next time, I won't pull you out.

Mike runs. Disappears.

Marco stands alone in the room. The glow pulses.

He puts his hand on the glass.

**MARCO**
(quietly)
Just like you taught me, Tony.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

**DR. RUSSO**
And how did it make you feel? This person. This student. Making this choice?

**TONY**
Proud.

**DR. RUSSO**
Proud?

**TONY**
That's the worst part.

**DR. RUSSO**
I don't understand.

**TONY**
I told him — I told this person — to find another way. Not the usual way. A different way. And they did. They found a way that was worse.

**DR. RUSSO**
Worse how?

**TONY**
Because it worked.

**DR. RUSSO**
That doesn't make sense.

**TONY**
It does if you've been doing this long enough. Sometimes the worst solution is the only one that works. And you have to live with that. Every day. Every time you look at them.

**DR. RUSSO**
This student. They did something terrible. And you're proud?

**TONY**
I'm proud because they did what I couldn't. They made the call. They crossed the line. And they did it for — for the family.

**DR. RUSSO**
(leaning forward)
Tony. What line?

**TONY**
The line I've been drawing for twenty years. The one I keep saying I'll never cross. The one that keeps moving.

**DR. RUSSO**
What line are you talking about?

**TONY**
(slowly)
The one that says I'm still a good person.

The room is quiet.

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony—

**TONY**
(standing)
I had a student. Once. A long time ago. And I taught him everything I know. And now he's better at it than I am.

**DR. RUSSO**
That's... that's a tragedy.

**TONY**
(small, broken smile)
That's a promotion.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. TANK ROOM - NIGHT**

Tony stands in front of the tank. Frank is there. Tony the lobster is there. All of them.

Marco enters. Stands next to him.

**MARCO**
It's done.

**TONY**
I know.

**MARCO**
You're mad.

**TONY**
I'm not mad.

**MARCO**
Then what?

**TONY**
I'm something else.

**MARCO**
What?

**TONY**
I'm wondering what happens when you cross a line that doesn't exist anymore.

**MARCO**
What do you mean?

**TONY**
I mean — I mean there's no line anymore. There's just — just the tank. And everyone who goes in. And everyone who comes out. And me. Standing here. Watching.

**MARCO**
That's not — that's not how it is.

**TONY**
No? Then how is it?

**MARCO**
It's — it's the code. What we do. Why we do it. The family.

**TONY**
(slowly)
The family.

**MARCO**
Yeah.

**TONY**
Which family, Marco? Ours? Or the one we just destroyed?

Marco doesn't answer.

**TONY**
You went too far.

**MARCO**
I did what you couldn't.

**TONY**
That's not a compliment.

**MARCO**
It's not an insult either.

They stand there. The glow pulses. The hum fills everything.

**TONY**
(finally)
Get out of here.

**MARCO**
Tony—

**TONY**
I said get out.

Marco leaves.

Tony stands alone.

---

### COLD CLOSE

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony. Before you go. I need to ask you something.

**TONY**
Yes?

**DR. RUSSO**
What happened to your student? This person who made the choice?

**TONY**
(pausing at the door)
They became the teacher.

**DR. RUSSO**
What do you mean?

**TONY**
I mean — I mean next time, I won't be the one giving the orders.

He leaves.

She stares at the door.

Her phone buzzes. Text from an unknown number: "Stop asking about the family. For your own safety."

She looks at the text. Puts it down.

Her hands are shaking.

---

**EXT. AGENTINO SANITATION - NIGHT**

Tony walks out. Gets in his Cadillac.

Sits there.

Takes out his phone. Texts Marco: "We need space. Don't contact me for a while."

Then: "You did what I couldn't. I'm not forgiveness yet."

He puts the phone down. Rubs his arm.

Looks at the wedding photo.

Pulls out.

---

**TITLE CARD:**

*"The mess is always there. You just move it around."*

**TONY AGENTINO**
**Episode 6: Marco's Choice**

## Notes for Production

- **Marco crosses the line:** He puts his own brother in the tank. Makes him talk for six hours. Then pulls him out. "Just like Tony taught us."
- **Tony's reaction:** Not anger. Pride. And horror. And the realization that he's created a monster.
- **The code breaks:** Tony says "There's no line anymore. There's just the tank."
- **Dr. Russo gets threatened:** Text from unknown number. "Stop asking about the family." She's in deeper than she knows.
- **The split:** Tony tells Marco "Don't contact me for a while." The partnership is fracturing.
- **Tony in therapy:** "I had a student... next time, I won't be the one giving the orders." He's passing the torch. Reluctantly.
- **Tease for Episode 7:** Donna Agentino has a stroke. Tony's mother. She knows something about the tank. About Tony's father.
- **The arm:** Still sore. Still nothing. The mystery deepens.
