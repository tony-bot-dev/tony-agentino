# Tony Agentino — Episode 5
## "The Glow"

**Length:** ~4 minutes  
**Format:** Cold open (Tony in therapy) → Cutaway → Therapy → Cutaway → Therapy → Cutaway → Cold close

---

### COLD OPEN

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

Tony sits in the chair. He looks like he hasn't slept in days. His eyes are red. His suit is the same one from yesterday.

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony. When did you last sleep?

**TONY**
(snapping back)
What?

**DR. RUSSO**
Sleep. When did you last sleep?

**TONY**
I sleep.

**DR. RUSSO**
When?

**TONY**
I — I've been dealing with something. At work. It's — there's something wrong with one of our facilities.

**DR. RUSSO**
Wrong how?

**TONY**
It's — it's making noise. At night. A hum. It's been going on for — I don't know how long.

**DR. RUSSO**
A hum?

**TONY**
A vibration. Like something's — like something's underneath. Underneath everything.

**DR. RUSSO**
That sounds unsettling.

**TONY**
(small laugh)
You have no idea.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. TANK ROOM - NIGHT**

The tank. Something's different now.

The water isn't just water anymore. There's a glow. Blue. Faint. Coming from somewhere deep.

Sal stands in front of it. He's holding a flashlight. Shaking.

**SAL**
(to himself)
This is insane. This is insane.

He leans closer. Looks into the water.

The lobsters are calm. Too calm. They're not moving. They're just — there. Floating. Like they're waiting.

**SAL**
(whispering)
What are you guys looking at?

A sound. Low. Deep. Coming from beneath the floor.

*THUMMMMMM.*

Sal drops the flashlight.

**SAL**
(backing up)
Okay. Okay. I'm leaving. I'm leaving.

He runs out.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

**DR. RUSSO**
You said "underneath everything." What did you mean by that?

**TONY**
I meant — I meant the building. The foundation. There's a basement. Under the basement.

**DR. RUSSO**
That's not unusual.

**TONY**
This one is.

**DR. RUSSO**
Why?

**TONY**
Because I've been in that basement for years. And I never knew — I never knew there was another one.

**DR. RUSSO**
Another basement?

**TONY**
Another floor. Under the floor. Under the concrete. Under everything.

**DR. RUSSO**
How did you find it?

**TONY**
(slowly)
The floor started to shake. Last week. Just — just a little. Just enough to notice. And when I went down to check —

He stops.

**DR. RUSSO**
What?

**TONY**
There was a door. That's been there the whole time. Behind a wall. Behind — behind everything.

**DR. RUSSO**
What was behind the door?

**TONY**
(long pause)
Something that's been there longer than I have.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. SECRET BASEMENT - NIGHT**

Tony stands in a room that shouldn't exist.

The walls are old. Brick. Covered in symbols that don't make sense. Faded. Ancient.

In the center of the room: a pool. Not a tank. A pool. Filled with water that glows blue.

And in the pool: lobsters. Hundreds of them. More than he knew existed.

The hum is louder here. It fills everything.

Tony walks closer. Looks down.

The lobsters look up at him.

One of them — the biggest one — moves toward him. Slowly. Like it knows him.

**TONY**
(whispering)
What are you?

The lobster doesn't answer. Of course it doesn't.

But it doesn't look away either.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony. You've been rubbing your arm again.

**TONY**
(his hand frozen)
Sorry.

**DR. RUSSO**
It's been happening more often. During our sessions. During our last few sessions.

**TONY**
I'm just — I'm just sore.

**DR. RUSSO**
From what?

**TONY**
From — from moving things. At work. Heavy things. We've been — we've been doing some renovations.

**DR. RUSSO**
This is the second time you've mentioned "renovations" this week.

**TONY**
It's a big project.

**DR. RUSSO**
A project.

**TONY**
An excavation.

**DR. RUSSO**
Excavating what?

**TONY**
(looking at her)
Something that's been buried for a long time.

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony. What are you not telling me?

**TONY**
(standing)
I think — I think I'm in over my head.

**DR. RUSSO**
About the basement?

**TONY**
About everything.

(beat)

I thought I knew what I was dealing with. I thought I understood the system. But there's — there's a whole level I've never seen. Underneath. Underneath everything I've built.

**DR. RUSSO**
That sounds terrifying.

**TONY**
(small, broken laugh)
It is. It really, really is.

---

### CUTAWAY

**INT. TANK ROOM - NIGHT**

Tony sits on the floor. His back against the tank. The hum surrounds him.

Sal enters. Sees Tony. Sits down next to him.

**SAL**
You look like shit.

**TONY**
I feel like shit.

**SAL**
The — the glow. It's getting stronger.

**TONY**
I know.

**SAL**
And the hum. I can hear it now. From upstairs. From the street. People are going to notice.

**TONY**
I know.

**SAL**
What are we going to do?

**TONY**
(looking at the tank)
I don't know anymore.

**SAL**
Tony.

**TONY**
Yeah?

**SAL**
I named one of them. The new one. After everything that happened. I named him.

**TONY**
Yeah?

**SAL**
I named him Tony.

Tony looks at Sal.

**TONY**
Why?

**SAL**
Because — because you're the only one who understands. The only one who gets it. The lobsters. The tank. The — the whole thing. And I thought —

His voice breaks.

**SAL**
I thought if there was a lobster named Tony, maybe he'd tell us what to do.

Tony is quiet for a long time.

**TONY**
(finally)
I'm not a lobster, Sal.

**SAL**
I know. But sometimes you act like one.

**TONY**
What do you mean?

**SAL**
You just — you sit there. In the water. Waiting for something to happen. Not moving. Not doing anything. Just — just waiting.

Tony looks at the tank. The glow. The hum.

**TONY**
Maybe that's all any of us do.

---

### COLD CLOSE

**INT. DR. RUSSO'S OFFICE - DAY**

Tony sits in the chair. He's cleaner now. Slept, maybe. But something's different. Something's shifted.

**DR. RUSSO**
You look better.

**TONY**
I feel better.

**DR. RUSSO**
What changed?

**TONY**
I stopped fighting it.

**DR. RUSSO**
Fighting what?

**TONY**
The hum.

**DR. RUSSO**
The hum?

**TONY**
I kept trying to ignore it. To work around it. To pretend it wasn't there. But it's always there. It's always been there. Underneath everything.

**DR. RUSSO**
And now?

**TONY**
And now I'm listening.

**DR. RUSSO**
Listening to what?

**TONY**
(looking at her)
To what's underneath.

She writes something down. Looks at him.

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony. Can I tell you something?

**TONY**
Of course.

**DR. RUSSO**
I looked into your family. Your father. He —

Tony freezes.

**DR. RUSSO**
He died in 1998. I found the obituary. But there was something — something strange about it.

**TONY**
What?

**DR. RUSSO**
The cause of death was listed as "industrial accident." At his place of work.

**TONY**
(very carefully)
It's a waste management company. Those happen.

**DR. RUSSO**
I know. But I also found a building permit. From 1997. For a basement renovation. At the same facility.

Tony is very, very still.

**DR. RUSSO**
Tony. What happened to your father?

**TONY**
(long silence)
I think — I think next week we should talk about something else.

**DR. RUSSO**
But—

**TONY**
(standing)
I'll see you next week.

He leaves.

She stares at the door.

Then at her notebook.

She writes: "His father. Something happened. Something under everything."

---

**EXT. AGENTINO SANITATION - NIGHT**

Tony walks out. Stops on the sidewalk.

Takes out his phone. Calls Sal.

**TONY**
Sal. The basement. Nobody goes down there. Nobody. Especially not her.

**SAL** (V.O.)
Who's her?

**TONY**
The doctor.

He hangs up.

Gets in the Cadillac.

Sits there.

Rubs his arm.

Looks at the wedding photo.

Pulls out.

---

**TITLE CARD:**

*"The mess is always there. You just move it around."*

**TONY AGENTINO**
**Episode 5: The Glow**

## Notes for Production

- **The secret deepens:** There's a basement under the basement. A pool. Ancient symbols. Hundreds of lobsters. The glow isn't just in the tank — it's everywhere.
- **Tony's unraveling:** He's not sleeping. The hum is getting to him. He's sitting on the floor of the tank room at 3am with Sal.
- **Sal named a lobster Tony:** This is huge. Sal's coping mechanism. His way of making sense of things.
- **Dr. Russo is digging:** She found the obituary. The industrial accident. The building permit. She's getting close.
- **The arm:** Still sore. Still rubbing it. Still no explanation.
- **Tease for Episode 6:** Marco's brother gets arrested. Federal. And Marco makes a choice that changes everything.
